ABOUT ME

Hey there! It's nice to meet you!

My name is Zachary Rosen. I'm a Data Scientist and computational linguist with expertise in Language Modeling (from LLMs to earlier word vector models), NLU methods for group/consumer insights, and user behavior modeling. I tend to rely on a gammut of tools--no problem requires quite the same modeling approach--but what thematically ties my work together is my emphasis on making sense of human behavior using big data.

If you're looking to learn a bit more about me, feel free to keep scrolling on this page, or check out my Curriculum Vitae in the link in the banner. You can get a quick summary of some of my more recent research initiatives via the "Current Initiatives" page (link in the banner). And if you want to read about some nerdy, pop-sci data science experiments, head on over to my blog.

Let me know if you have any questions or want to collaborate on a particular project or idea. You can reach me by email at zrosen[at]uci.edu.
